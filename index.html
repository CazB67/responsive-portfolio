About Me

Welcome! I am a Full Stack Web Developer leveraging a background in special education and primary teaching to build accessible and responsive applications for teachers of and students with additional teaching and learning needs. Recently earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from the University of Western Australia Coding Bootcamp, with newly developed skills in JavaScript, CSS, JQuery, Node js. CLI and responsive web design. Known as organised and hard working with a flair for the creative, I hope to create visually appealing user-friendly and useful applications. I have worked in a team of three to successfully develop a Find Surf Spot app that helps users to locate surf spots around Perth based on weather conditions and their surfing ability. Excited to use my newly learnt skills to develop applications that solve existing problems and exceed clients’ expectations.

My hobbies include running, hiking and camping with my family. I also enjoy spending time at the beach and trying out new recipes.

Please enjoy perusing my portfolio as it grows through time and as my knowledge and skills develop.
